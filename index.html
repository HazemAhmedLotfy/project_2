about us

Design House - your best choice for Architect Service.

Design House was founded in 2018 in Cairo, by a group of friends. Since then we’ve made more than 1000 images for our customers over the whole world. From North and South America through Europe, to the Middle East, Asia and Australia. The quality of our work was appreciated on our Behance profile, where we’ve gathered 9 rewards, 100k+ views, and 10k+likes. Thank you for your all support. We don’t stop yet, the sky is not the limit – perhaps one day we’ll be able to visualize a project on Mars…
